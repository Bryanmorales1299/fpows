1. Executive Overview

The FPOWS (Fire Protection Outstanding Works Status) platform is a sophisticated automation layer built on top of the simPRO API. It is designed to modernize how Redmen Fire Protection tracks compliance, manages client portfolios, and generates legal reporting documents.

By shifting from manual spreadsheets to real-time data synchronization, the system ensures that critical fire safety defects are prioritized, reported, and quoted with zero latency.

2. Target Audience

Customer Experience Agents (CEA) Primary users for high-volume site lookups, job verification, and dispatching official "Call Sheets" to clients.
Portfolio Managers Utilized to audit the compliance health of "Major Clients" and identify sites requiring immediate intervention.
Sales & Account Teams Used as a lead-generation tool to identify sites with long periods of inactivity or outstanding quotes.
Compliance Officers To ensure AFSS (Annual Fire Safety Statement) critical items are being addressed within legal timeframes.

3. Key Benefits

  • Drastic Time Savings: Automated report generation reduces a 2-hour manual data entry task to a 2-minute automated sync.
  • 100% Data Accuracy: By pulling data directly from simPRO, the risks associated with manual "copy-pasting" (IDs, Site Names, Addresses) are eliminated.
  • Compliance Safety Net: Dynamic overdue flags (calculated to exact months and days) ensure that no site remains inactive without a visible alert.
  • Professional Brand Image: Standardized, premium call sheets and emails provide clients with a consistent and high-quality interaction experience.
  • Collaborative Visibility: Live indicators allow multiple agents to see who else is viewing a job, preventing duplicate emails to the same client.

4. Automated Functions

The core of FPOWS lies in its "Set and Forget" logic. Several key business functions have been fully automated:

A. Real-Time simPRO Sync

The system automatically connects to simPRO to retrieve Job Status, Site Metadata, Equipment Types, and DARN/Lead/Quote linkages without user input.

B. Dynamic Overdue & Aging Logic

Using a sub-second calculation, the system computes the exact time since the last site touchpoint. It identifies customers who are Overdue (30+ days of inactivity) and calculates the age down to the exact day.

C. Intelligent Client Prioritization

The software automatically scans simPRO customer tags. Clients tagged with "Major Contract," "Testing," or "Non-Testing" are automatically prioritized and pinned to the top of the search dashboard.

D. Call Sheet Report Generation

Professional fire safety reports (PDF/HTML) are generated instantly by scraping live data. This includes automated "Clean Descriptions" which strip away internal technical shorthand to present a clean "Client-Facing" narrative.

E. Automated Asset Date Discovery

The system deep-dives into Customer Assets to find the most accurate 6-Monthly and 12-Monthly service due dates, providing a "Source of Truth" for AFSS compliance.

5. Operational Workflow

FPOWS follows a simplified 4-step workflow:

  1. Discovery: Use the Global Search to find clients by name, ID, or Postcode.
  2. Verification: Expand the customer to see all recent work (Pending through Invoiced).
  3. Aggregation: Click into a job to pull the full site history and outstanding works list.
  4. Dispatch: Review the automated report and click "Publish" to email the Call Sheet to the client and manager.

6. Data Integrity & Security

All data is processed through secure infrastructure. Access tokens for simPRO are never exposed to the client-side, and all communications are encrypted. The system includes an automated "Email Lock" during development stages to prevent accidental live emails to clients.
